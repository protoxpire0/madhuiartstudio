Art Classes

All children are artists. The problem is how to remain an artist once he grows up.

-Pablo Picasso

Basic Drawing Skills

The classes focus on developing the students' strengths in drawing and painting. Basic drawing skills are taught, which include shading, understanding proportion, shapes and lines. The students are also taught to properly use materials such as pastels, watercolour , acrylic paint and oil paint.

Enjoy While Learning

Madhuri Art Studio has three levels that the students will be placed in. The art teacher places the students in the levels depending on their ability to draw. The three levels are:

- Beginner Level
- Intermediate Level
- Advanced Level

In each class, students will be taught three drawings which they will also have for homework. It is important to complete homework every week to see improvements.

Watch Your Child Grow as an Artist

Every month Madhuri art studio has exhibitions to showcase the student artworks. Parents and guardians are welcome to attend the events to see the progress your children are making in arts. Madhuri Art Studio holds art competitions and exhibitions. Students attending Madhuri Art Studio has the opportunity to volunteer or be employed in the future .
